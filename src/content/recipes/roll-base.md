---
title: "Roll base"
description: "Parker house rolls are a great extension"
pubDate: "May 24 2023"
prep: "10 min"
cook: "2 hours"
yield: "8 scones"
ingredients:
  - "1/4 cup warm water"
  - "1 tbs yeast"
  - "1 cup milk, scalded"
  - "1/4 cup sugar"
  - "1/4 cup shortening"
  - "1 tsp salt"
  - "3 1/2 cups ap flour (465 g)"
  - "1 egg"
instructions:
  - "Combine milk shortening, salt and sugar"
  - "Mix with 1 1/2 cup (200 g) flour"
  - "Add egg and softened yeast"
  - "Add remaining flour"
  - "Rise for 1 hour until doubled"
  - "Form rolls"
  - "Rise for 30 min"
  - "Bake at 400 F for 15-22 min"
tags: ["staple"]
---
